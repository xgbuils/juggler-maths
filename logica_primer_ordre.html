Lògica de primer ordre

  1. Regles distributives entre quantificadors i connectives

    1. $p(y) \land \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{p(y) \land q(x)}$, sempre que $X \ne \emptyset$
    2. $p(y) \land \Exists{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Exists{x \in X}{p(y) \land q(x)}$
    3. $p(y) \lor \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{p(y) \lor q(x)}$
    4. $p(y) \lor \Exists{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Exists{x \in X}{p(y) \lor q(x)}$, sempre que $X \ne \emptyset$

  2. Regles deduïdes de les anteriors

    1. $\Forall{y \in Y}{p(y)} \land \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{\Forall{y \in Y}{p(y) \land q(x)}}$, sempre que $X \ne \emptyset \land Y \ne \emptyset$
    2. $\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)} \land \Exists{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Exists{x \in X}{\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y) \land q(x)}}$
    3. $\Forall{y \in Y}{p(y)} \lor \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{\Forall{y \in Y}{p(y) \lor q(x)}}$
    4. $\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)} \lor \Exists{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Exists{x \in X}{\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y) \lor q(x)}}$, sempre que $X \ne \emptyset \land Y \ne \emptyset$
    5. $\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)} \land \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)\land q(x)}} \equiv \Exists{y \in Y}{\Forall{x \in X}{p(y)\land q(x)}}$, sempre que $X \ne \emptyset$
    6. $\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)} \lor \Forall{x \in X}{q(x)} \equiv \Forall{x \in X}{\Exists{y \in Y}{p(y)\lor q(x)}} \equiv \Exists{y \in Y}{\Forall{x \in X}{p(y)\lor q(x)}}$, sempre que $Y \ne \emptyset$
